To measure the distance between the eyes and the computer screen. I chose to use an ultrasonic sensor. Use the formula distance=duration*sound speed/2 to calculate the distance, and upload the distance (cm) to io.

The data graph of the feed in io is the distance between the sensor and the computer screen when I use the computer for an hour.

In the actual connection, due to the need to convert the voltage and portability, it is necessary to connect an external battery as a power source and use a voltage converter.

Wearing method: I made a wearable device made of cardboard that can be stuck on my glasses, and the battery and motherboard can be placed on the back. Design a circular pattern composed of constantly moving circles in p5 to show a more pupil-like visual effect, and control the radius of the entire large circle through the collected data to zoom in or out.
